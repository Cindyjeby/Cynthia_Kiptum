Goal

The primary goal of the 100SMILES Dental Care website is to increase new patient bookings by 20% within the next six months. This will be achieved by converting website visitors into patients by prominently featuring the "20% discount on your first dental visit" as a clear call-to-action, which encourages users to book their first appointment via email.The site will also serve to enhance the business's online presence and credibility.

Success Evaluation

Success will be evaluated by tracking the primary goal. We will measure the number of new patient booking emails sent to admin@100smilesdentalcare.com.au that specifically reference the "20% first visit" website promotion. This provides a direct, quantifiable metric for new patient conversions. We will compare the monthly number of these conversion emails post-launch against a pre-launch baseline to track progress toward the 20% goal.

Target Audience

1. Target Audience: Our primary target audience is adults and parents (aged 30-50) in the Razole area who are looking for a reliable, "all-in-one" dental provider for their families.These are typically busy individuals who value trust, experience, and the convenience of finding a single clinic that can handle everything from a child's first check-up to adult cosmetic dentistry.

2. Intentional Design: The website will be intentionally designed for this audience by:

  • Building Trust:Prominently featuring the "Our Staff" section and the clinic's 30+ years of community history to establish credibility and a personal connection.
  • Clarity and Efficiency:Making contact information, trading hours, and the "Book Now" call-to-action (for the 20% discount) highly visible on every page, so a busy parent can find it quickly.
  • Family-Oriented Content: Clearly organising the comprehensive services to show they cover all family needs (e.g., Orthodontics, Check-ups, Implants), and using provided images that reflect a welcoming, family-friendly environment.
